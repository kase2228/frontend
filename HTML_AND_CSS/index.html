Minilik II Hospital, established in 1909, is one of the oldest hospitals in Ethiopia. It's a public health care facility located in Addis Ababa, serving as a tertiary care hospital that provides specialized services. The hospital is administered by the Addis Ababa City Administration and has a capacity of over 800 beds.

The hospital offers a wide range of specialties including cardiology, neurology, oncology, and other fields. It is known for its tertiary eye care and forensic medicine services. A recent public-private partnership has helped the hospital open a kidney transplant and dialysis center.
