Neurology

Neurology involves the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the nervous system. This includes diseases of the brain, spinal cord, nerves, and muscles.

Bones

Orthopedics is the medical specialty that focuses on injuries and diseases of your body's musculoskeletal system. This includes bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, and muscles.

Oncology

Oncology is a branch of medicine that deals with the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of cancer.

Otorhinolaryngology

Otorhinolaryngology, also known as ENT, is the branch of medicine that specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the ear, nose, and throat.

Ophthalmology

Ophthalmology is a branch of medicine and surgery which deals with the diagnosis and treatment of eye disorders.

Cardiovasculars

Cardiology is a branch of medicine that deals with the disorders of the heart as well as some parts of the cardiovascular system.

Pulmonology

Pulmonology is a medical specialty that deals with diseases involving the respiratory tract.

Renal Medicine

Renal medicine also called, Nephrology is a specialty of medicine and pediatrics that concerns itself with the kidneys: the study of normal kidney function and kidney disease, the preservation of kidney health, and the treatment of kidney disease, from diet and medication to renal replacement therapy.

Gastroenterology

Gastroenterology is the branch of medicine focused on the digestive system and its disorders.

Urology

Urology is a part of health care that deals with diseases of the male and female urinary tract (kidneys, ureters, bladder and urethra). It also deals with the male organs that are able to make babies (penis, testes, scrotum, prostate, etc.).

Dermatology

Dermatology is the branch of medicine dealing with the skin, nails, hair and its diseases. It is a specialty with both medical and surgical aspects.

Gynaecology

Gynaecology or gynecology is the medical practice dealing with the health of the female reproductive system (uterus and ovaries).
